Instructions

This is a simple speed typing practice-game

The objective

  • You will be presented with numerous words passed on to you one at a time
  • Type the given word as fast as possible, there is limited time
  • Once your input matches the given word, you will be given a new word
  • Make sure you type the word exactly as it is given
  • There is a timer counting down and you should finish typing all the words before it hits zero
  • For each word successfully typed a few seconds will be added to the countdown, extending the time you have to finish the rest of the session

Setup settings

  • Various levels of difficulty can be set according to your skill. They impact:
    • The amount of time given at the start
    • The amount of time added upon each successfully typed word
  • In addition to the difficulty, other settings can be tuned to your preference as well:
    • Language of the words
    • The number of characters each word has
    • The amount of words required to be typed

Score

  • Your average typing time per word can be seen in the top right corner
  • The scoreboard is ranked on sessions that have the shortest average time of typed words

To abort the game, press the exit button in the upper left corner
